Michael and Michelle’s March Madness


OUR HISTORY


Michael and Michelle’s March Madness was originally founded in West Lafayette, IN in 2018.

Michael and Michelle are both Purdue graduates that have enjoyed following Purdue Basketball over the years. Because of this, they have been witness to some memorable March Madness runs which has deepened their love of the sport. In 2018, they founded Michael and Michelle’s March Madness to share this passion with others. They offer a variety of things from basketball jerseys to youth camps for all ages.Their store location has been successfully running for nearly 4 years near Purdue’s campus.

Important dates about our business
  • June 2018: Lafayette store location opening

  • March 2019: First youth camp held in Indianapolis

  • October 2020: Became a sponsor for Purdue Athletics
