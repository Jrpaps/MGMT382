Meet the Team


These are the employees of Michael and Michelle’s March Madness!






Michael Scott - Co-owner
Michael Scott is one of the co-owners of Michaels and Michelles March madness. Here are some of his responsibilities:
  • Michael is in charge of Planning and Strategy for this business
  • He developed a business plan alongside his sister and revisits and changes as needed
  • He also overlooks the rest of the employees

Michelle Scott - Co-owner
Michelle Scott is one of the other co-owners of Michaels and Michelles March madness. Here are some of her responsibilities:
  • Michelle is in charge of compliance and legal responsibilities
  • She is in charge of writing, reviewing and signing contracts and ensures the business complies with federal and state business licensing laws
  • She also overlooks the other employees along with Michael


Pam Beesly - Customer Service Associate
Pam Beesly is the Customer Service Associate of this small business. Here are some of her responsibilities:
  • Pam ensures that all customers are satisfied by their products and services
  • She has a vide array of responsibilities from phone calls to emails and follow ups with customers
  • She also helps with the front desk whenever necessary

Dwight Schrute - Marketing and Sales Associate
Dwight Schrute is the Marketing and Sales Associate of this small business. Here are some of his responsibilities:
  • Dwight implements marketing and sales strategies
  • He prints advertising, flyers and also takes part in online marketing
  • Public relations and networking is also a big responsibility he undertakes

Jim Halpert - Sales Intern
Jim Halpert is the sales intern of this small business. Here are some of his responsibilities:
  • He is responsible for helping customers with their needs
  • He also puts up flyers and updates the marketing wall with new promotions
  • He also helps out at the front desk
